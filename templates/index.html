Reduce Disease

Many studies agree that a vegetarian diet can offer a range of health benefits. Studies show that a vegan or vegetarian diet may reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease and various types of cancer.

vegetarians are growing

A growing number of younger people are adopting a vegetarian diet, as the Gallup poll shows. They may do this because:

  • it brings health benefits
  • it is a more ecologically sustainable option
  • they have concerns about the treatment of animals
  • it is part of a broader lifestyle choice
  • Some people also avoid meat and animal products for religious reasons.
